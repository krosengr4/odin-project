***** THE BOX MODEL *****

- The most important skills to know when it comes to HTML and CSS:
    1. Positioning
    2. Layout

- The Box Model
    - Every single thing on a webpage is a rectangular box.

- Manipulating Size
    - Padding:
        - Increases the space beween the border of a box and the content of the box.
    - Border:
        - Adds space between the margin and the padding.
    - Margin:
        - Increases the space between the borders of a box and the borders of adjacent boxes.
