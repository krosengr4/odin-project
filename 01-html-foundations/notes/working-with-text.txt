*** WORKING WITH TEXT ***

- Paragraphs
    - Each paragraph needs to be seperated with the <p> tag.

- Headings
    - Headings are displayed larger and bolder than other text to signify they are headings.
    - There are 6 different types from <h1> to <h6>.

- Bold(strong) Element
    - The <strong> makes the text bold.
    - You can use <strong> on its own but will probably use it in combo with other elements.

- Italic(em) Element
    - The <em> element makes text italic.
